A free web-site to detect Covid-19 from your chest X-rays

This web-site aims to detect the presence of Covid-19 automatically using your digital chest x-ray images. The web-site uses a deep-learning model (based on CNN architecture), for an accurate prediction. All you have to do is just upload your chest x-ray image in either PNG or JPG format, and we'll do the rest.

Disclaimer: Our deep-learning model is trained to be accurate by 92.19%. In case our website detects your sample to be positive, it is adviced to take a second opinion from proper medical institions. Similarly, in case the result from here is negative but you exhibit signs of covid-19 infection, it is again recommended to check with some proper medical institions.
